Overview

Heda is a versatile migraine diary that helps you track your migraine episodes, symptoms, triggers, and medications. It integrates with Google Health Connect to let you read and write health data directly from your Android device.

How to use the app (general)

  • Log an episode – record a migraine episode with onset time, pain level, location, symptoms, and any medications taken.
  • Track triggers – add potential triggers (sleep, stress, weather, diet, etc.) to help identify patterns over time.
  • View history – browse your diary entries by day, week, or month to see trends in your migraine frequency and severity.
  • Health Connect – enable the Google Health Connect integration in Settings to sync migraine data with other health apps on your device.
  • Settings – configure notifications, reminders, Health Connect permissions, and privacy options.

Features

Migraine Diary
Log episodes with pain level, duration, symptoms, aura, and notes.
Trigger Tracking
Record and review potential triggers to identify personal patterns.
Medication Log
Track acute and preventive medications and their effectiveness.
Statistics & History
View charts and summaries of your migraine frequency and severity over time.
Google Health Connect
Read and write health data on your device via the Android Health Connect platform.
